Executive Board:
Our executives are entrusted with the strategic guidance and execution of the association's objectives while ensuring tactical implementation is done synonymously with the members and the communities at large in a true spirit of servant-leadership

The executive board consist of the:

  • President

  • Vice President

  • Secretary

  • Assistant Secretary

  • Financial Secretary

  • Assistant Financial Secretary

  • Treasurer

  • Publicity Secretary/Provost
