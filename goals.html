Our Objetives

We aim to provide a platform for interaction and fostering of unity, mutual respect, understanding and harmony among Akanu Ohafia indigenes resident in the USA, and to promote and preserve Akanu Ohafia cultural heritage, acceptable by the Association.

Overall purpose of this Association is, primarily, to advance Akanu Ohafia cultural, overall charitable and vital information-sharing activities in the USA and in Akanu Ohafia, Abia State, Nigeria. More specifically, the objectives of the Association are:

  1. To provide a platform for interaction and fostering of unity, mutual respect, understanding and harmony among Akanu Ohafia indigenes resident in the USA.
  2. To promote and preserve Akanu Ohafia cultural heritage, acceptable by the Association.
  3. To foster the welfare of the Association’s members and their immediate family members. In this Constitution, immediate family members refer to husband, wife, children who are biological or legally-adopted, parents, and biological brothers and sisters. Children and brothers and sisters shall be under the age of 25 years and resident in the USA.
  4. To engage in community projects when feasible and as shall be agreed upon by the Association; i.e., projects that shall foster cultural and social wellbeing amongst members of the community in the USA and in Akanu Ohafia.
  5. For members to use their time, talent and resources to help the needy in the community here in the USA as well as in Akanu Ohafia—the needy as shall be defined on occurrence, and acceptable by majority of active members present and voting at a well-constituted general assembly.
  6. To encourage and promote the exchange of ideas and information in order to enhance understanding between the community and members of the Association.
  7. To help disseminate Akanu Ohafia cultural heritage, tradition and custom to other Associations with similar interests.
